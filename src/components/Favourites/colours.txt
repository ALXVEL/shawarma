Color Usage
Warm Red: Use for accents, call-to-action buttons, and important highlights.
Creamy Beige: Ideal for backgrounds, creating a clean and inviting look.
Olive Green: Great for secondary elements, headers, or subtle accents.
Deep Brown: Suitable for text, footers, and any elements needing a solid base.
